# Moves Documentation


## Waterfall out of combat
The field move watefall, out of combat, is always permetted when the movement is downward, even without the normal requirements of the move.  
The change was made to simplify the player navigation around the map without any drawback since the watefall blockages are always from down to up and never the other way around.  


## Forgottable HM moves
All HM moves can be overwritten by any other move without the need of the Move Deleter.  
The change was made to remove the annoyances related to using the Move Deleter NPC. If the player is dumb enough to soft-lock themselves even after the [field moves changes](field_moves.md), so be it.
